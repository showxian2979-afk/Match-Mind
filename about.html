About Match-Mind

Match-Mind is a Smart Dating Platform prototype created as part of a university project. It demonstrates how technology and design thinking can make human connection smarter, safer, and more inclusive.

Our Mission

Our goal is to help people connect based on shared hobbies, interests, and values rather than appearances alone. We focus on promoting respectful communication, data privacy, and user well-being.
